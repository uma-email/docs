AEMS helps organizations take control of email through the use of advanced authorization mechanism User-Managed Access (UMA). Each email consists of resources (message and attachments) stored in an UMA Mailbox, protected by the UMA protocol. This architecture increases the robustness and performance of the existing mail system. From the user's point of view, AEMS looks like a standard mail system. People can use AEMS to store and exchange messages, documents, images, audios and videos.
